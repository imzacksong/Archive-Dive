# Archive Dive

**Doomscroll the Past.**

Archive Dive is a unique video/audio streaming experience that brings the vast, eccentric, and historical collection of the **Internet Archive** to your mobile device in a modern, vertical video feed. Think of it as "TikTok for the 20th Century."

Discover lost films, vintage commercials, educational reels, silent movies, and bizarre ephemeral footage tailored to your interests.

## 📺 The Experience
Forget the algorithm updates and trending dances. Archive Dive connects you directly with the past.
-   **Endless Vintage Feed**: Swipe through a bottomless pit of history. From the 1890s to the 2000s, experience media as it was.
-   **"Variety Sort" Engine**: Our custom randomization logic allows you to pick your favorite genres (Sci-Fi, Horror, Animation, etc.) and ensures a diverse mix of content, so you never get stuck watching the same type of clip twice in a row.
-   **No Account Needed**: Just launch, pick your tags, and start diving.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🔍 Discovery & Personalization
-   **Onboarding Tag Selection**: Choose from over 30 categories like *Noir, Documentary, Anime, Animation, Sci-Fi, Industrial, and more*.
-   **Smart Filters**: Want to see only 1980s commercials? Or French cinema? Filter your feed by **Decade** (1800s - 2020s) and **Language**.
-   **Favorites**: Heart the weirdest and best clips to save them to your personal Saved collection.

### 🎥 Modern Player, Retro Content
-   **Vertical Swipe Navigation**: The familiar, intuitive navigation you love, applied to history's archives.
-   **Gesture Controls**:
    -   **Double Tap sides** to rewind (-10s) or fast forward (+30s).
    -   **Horizontal Swipe** to navigate multi-part videos (playlists).
-   **Source Intel**: Found something fascinating? Tap "Web" to open the original Internet Archive page in your browser for deeper research.
-   **Comments/Reviews**: Added a "comment section" that is read only. Pulled directly from the Internet Archive. Users can use the "Web" button to leave a comment and sign into their Archive account.
-   **NEW MUSIC MODE**: All of the features the video player has but only streaming audio and themed green.

### 🛠️ Under the Hood
-   **Privacy Focused**: No tracking, no specialized algorithms harvesting your data.
-   **Performance**: Background buffering and aggressive preloading ensure instant playback even for old file formats.
-   **Spam Protection**: Built-in filters to exclude repetitive automated uploads.
